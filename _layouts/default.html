About this site

First, this site serves a kind of journal. After all, it's nice to see your progress. Secondly, I'm hoping that the articles are helpful to others. I've made a career for myself by using knowledge that other people took the time to write down and post to the web. Time to give back a little.

Under the hood

  • Ubuntu - A little server running Ubuntu hosted over at Linode.
